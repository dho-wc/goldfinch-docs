## Product information

### Quick, simple, and affordable
Quickly create your state-specific will with our simple platform. Our AI assistant guides you, gathers the key information needed to create your will, and answers your questions along the way. Once complete, your will is automatically generated and ready to sign.

### Designed by attorneys
Our online will package has been designed by experienced attorneys to ensure your documents are legally sound and cover all essentials, including asset distribution, guardianship of children, and decisions about your finances and health care if you are unable to make them.

### Free updates for 12 months
Life changes, and your will should change with it. From major milestones to smaller adjustments in your circumstances, our platform gives you the flexibility to make unlimited updates for 12 months at no extra cost, ensuring your will remains accurate and up to date.

### Wills for spouses
For spouses, our mirror will option makes it easy to create two matching document sets in one simple process. The same selections you made will be used but reflected to swap you and your spouse where appropriate.
